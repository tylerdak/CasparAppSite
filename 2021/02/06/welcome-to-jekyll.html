Announcing Caspar:
An app for aspiring dog lovers!

Tyler Dakin

06 Feb 2021


Hello and welcome to what I hope will be a large library of updates for Caspar!

Caspar is an upcoming iOS app for people looking for dog names. There are several planned features to target dog lovers specifically and to make the app an easy-to-use experience for anyone looking to name their new addition to the family.

What will the app do?

I will be collecting several (looks like thousands) of dog names during the development period of this app and throwing them into a database.

When a user logs onto the app, they’ll get started with their first suggested name, coming from the database I end up with. At this point, the user can decide to keep the name for later, in a list tied to their account, or they can choose not to keep it. The user will make this decision with the familiar interface that Tinder uses. If you’re unfamiliar, it’s a simple system where you will swipe left to decline a name and swipe right to keep the name.

Now, at this point you may be thinking that this is a fairly simple idea and you’d be right! Apps like this have already been made! However, the apps I’ve seen so far target general pets. That’s fine, but I’d like to specifically target dog lovers in a few ways.

Here are a few of the features I’ve come up with to set my app apart from the rest:

  • Above every name shown will be a dog so you can imagine what it will be like to have the suggested name attached to your dog, even if you don’t have them yet.
    • In addition, you’ll be able to specify the breed of dog you’re getting and only see dogs of that breed!
  • Users will make an account upon first downloading the app. With this account, they can save their lists and compare them with other users!
    • If you and your partner are getting a dog together, you’ll be able to compare lists and see if you’ve agreed on any! (I plan to give paired users similar lists so they’ll actually see the same names)
  • Users will be able to suggest names to add to the database! If you’ve come up with a great name and want to share it with our users, you’ll be able to do so!

I have a complaint, question, or comment!

See if it’s one of these before asking me directly:

Is it only for iOS?

It might come to iPad, Apple Watch, and macOS depending on how nice the design turns out, but no promises. No Android support though. I consider myself an Apple platforms developer and I don’t plan on changing that unless I get really bored with the Apple ecosystem. I suggest Android users look at alternatives.

Will it be free?

I’m not sure yet. My last app was free, but that was because I didn’t see there being much demand for it. Depending on the attention these updates get and the work I put into this project, there might be a price attached. Ideally, after I publish this app, I’ll want to keep supporting it. My motivation to support an app will be much higher if I’m getting paid to do so. :P

When will it be released?

I’m absolutely not giving any estimate on a release date! My first app was pushed by 6+ months from the original release date. I’ll announce the release date when the app is ready for release. However, there WILL be a beta test period before the release. So if you want to be a part of that, I recommend you follow the TikTok account, Twitter, and/or RSS feed of this site to stay posted (RSS feed linked at the bottom of this page).

If none of these were your question, feel free to ask!

I’m very excited to begin work on this app and especially excited to see reactions to it. Thank you!
